A web for the next paradigm

As smart devices shrink to fit on our wrists, on our eyeglasses, and eventually embedded inside of us, the time we spend on the internet transitions from discrete, conscious interactions to a continuous experience. The Geo Web’s user experience model and network economics are designed for this future.

Shared Reality

Our lives lived through smartphones share little resemblance with each other because of siloed apps, algorithmic feeds, and the attention economy. As the internet pervades our physical world, the Geo Web can bring us back to a shared reality.

Prosocial Outcomes

The Geo Web puts the individual user in control of their data and their attention. The Geo Web’s unique property allocation market encourages egalitarian economic outcomes and the ability to directly fund social goods.

Decentralized Power

The internet is the defining medium of the Information Age. Foundational components are too important to be controlled and monetized by a small elite. The Geo Web is created and will be managed as an open-source public good.
